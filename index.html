How RegulaCore Solutions Works

RegulaCore Solutions transforms regulatory compliance into a structured, automated governance system by combining obligation intelligence, evidence-based validation, and real-time risk monitoring for UK SMEs.

01

Obligation Identification Engine

The platform automatically analyses business data such as VAT status, employee count, payroll cycles, and turnover to generate all applicable statutory obligations. This removes the need for regulatory knowledge and ensures nothing is missed.

02

Task Assignment & Workflow Structuring

Each obligation is converted into structured tasks and assigned to responsible roles within the organisation. The system ensures accountability and maintains continuity even if team members change.

03

Evidence-Based Compliance Execution

Tasks cannot be marked complete without verified evidence such as HMRC confirmations, reports, or payment receipts. This ensures compliance is not assumed but proven and audit-ready at all times.

04

Real-Time Risk & Dependency Monitoring

The system continuously evaluates compliance status and detects cascading risks across obligations. If one task is delayed, it automatically identifies downstream impact, helping businesses act before penalties occur.

RegulaCore Solutions Platform

RegulaCore Solutions is a digital compliance and financial governance platform designed for UK SMEs. It introduces a structured compliance intelligence layer that automates obligation management, validates execution with evidence, and provides real-time risk visibility across business operations.

Automated Obligation Engine

No manual compliance tracking.

The platform automatically generates statutory obligations based on business data such as VAT status, payroll cycles, turnover, and employee count. It continuously updates obligations as business conditions change, removing the need for regulatory knowledge.

Evidence-Based Compliance System

Compliance that is provable.

Tasks cannot be marked complete without validated evidence such as HMRC confirmations, payment receipts, or official reports. This ensures every compliance action is verifiable and audit-ready at all times.

Cascade Risk Intelligence

Detect risks before penalties.

The platform models dependencies between obligations. A delay in one task (e.g., payroll submission) automatically triggers risk signals in related areas such as pensions, tax filings, and statutory reports.

Role-Based Responsibility System

Clear ownership, no confusion.

Each obligation is assigned to specific roles within the business. Responsibility persists even when staff change, ensuring continuity and eliminating accountability gaps.

Real-Time Compliance Dashboard

Full visibility at all times.

Provides a founder-level dashboard showing compliance status, risk exposure, and audit readiness in real time, enabling faster and informed decision-making.

Jurisdiction-Ready Architecture

Built to scale globally.

The platform separates regulatory logic from execution, allowing expansion across regions (UK, EU, US) without rebuilding the system, making it future-ready for global compliance.
